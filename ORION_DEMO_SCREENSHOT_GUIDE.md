# ORION Screenshot & Media Capture Guide

This guide provides specific recommendations for capturing the most impactful User Interface (UI) states for your ORION presentation. A great demo combines wide-angle shots for context and close-ups for "hero" details.

---

## üì∏ Capture Preparation Tips
- **Resolution**: Capture at **1920x1080** for a standard slide deck.
- **Browser**: Use Chrome/Edge. Hide bookmarks bar (`Ctrl+Shift+B`) and use Full Screen mode (`F11`).
- **Clean Data**: Ensure no "test" or "asdf" data is visible. Use the "Reset Demo Data" feature if available.
- **Role Toggle**: Always start in **Ops Manager** role unless specified.

## **Phase 1.1: Product Assortment**

### 1. **The Staging View** (Wide Shot)
- **What**: The full screen showing the "PLM Staging" table with yellow "PENDING" badges.
- **Why**: Shows the starting point - data is ready but not yet in our system.

### 2. **The Sync Moment** (Hero Close-up)
- **What**: Close-up of the **"Register All Products"** button and the success alert text.
- **Why**: Highlights the "one-click" automation.

### 3. **The Active Catalog** (Result Shot)
- **What**: The Product Master table at the bottom with green "ACTIVE" badges.
- **Why**: Proves the transition from external data to our internal master data.

## **Phase 1.2: Volume Forecast**

### 4. **Parameter Configuration** (Detail Shot)
- **What**: The top configuration panel showing settings like "12 weeks", "85% confidence".
- **Why**: Shows that the system is configurable and "smart."

### 5. **Forecast Analysis** (Wide Shot)
- **What**: The table filled with demand projections, especially showing the **weekly breakdown columns**.
- **Why**: Visualizes the complexity of the data ORION is handling.

## **Phase 1.3: Supply Plan**

### 6. **The Draft Plan** (Wide Shot)
- **What**: The supply plan table with "DRAFT" status. Highlight the **Client Code** column (PSS_FR, PSS_DE, etc.).
- **Why**: Shows the consolidation from various international clients.

### 7. **Status Transition** (Hero Close-up)
- **What**: A "Before and After" of a status badge changing from **DRAFT (Grey)** ‚Üí **CALCULATED (Blue)**.
- **Why**: Signifies the completion of the calculation engine run.

## üé¨ Video/GIF Recording Suggestions

If your presentation tool supports video or GIFs, capture these 3 high-impact interactions:

1. **The Registration Sync** (3 seconds): Click "Register All" ‚Üí Watch badges turn green instantly.
2. **The "Wow" Moment** (12 seconds): Click "Batch Confirmation" ‚Üí Watch the banner appear ‚Üí Scroll down as 600 rows turn green one by one.
3. **The Role Swap**: Clicking the toggle and seeing the table structure shift seamlessly.
