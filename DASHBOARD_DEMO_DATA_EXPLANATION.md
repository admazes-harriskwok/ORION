### 2. **Shipment Negotiations**
**Current Value:** 18 active negotiations  
**Trend:** Active

**What it means:**
- Number of shipment proposals currently in the negotiation phase between Supplier and Ops
- Includes shipments with status:
  - `RELEASED_TO_SUPPLIER`: Ops has released proposal to supplier for review
  - `MOD_REQUESTED`: Supplier has requested modifications (PBSUP action)

**Business Impact:**

- High numbers may suggest:
  - Capacity constraints requiring ETD adjustments
  - Quantity mismatches between forecast and actual production
  - Port/routing optimization discussions
- Low numbers indicate smooth alignment between parties

**Related Workflow:** Steps 1.6.3 - 1.6.4 (Shipment Negotiation)

### 3. **Container Utilization**
**Current Value:** 87%  
**Trend:** Optimal

**What it means:**
- Average percentage of container space being utilized across all active shipments
- Calculated as: (Actual CBM / Maximum Container CBM) Ã— 100
- Industry benchmark: 75-85% is considered optimal

**Business Impact:**
- **85-95%:** Optimal - Maximizing shipping efficiency without overpacking
- **70-85%:** Acceptable - Room for consolidation improvements
- **Below 70%:** Suboptimal - Wasting shipping costs, consider consolidation
- **Above 95%:** Risk of damage, customs issues, or rejected containers

**Cost Implications:**
- Every 10% improvement in utilization can reduce shipping costs by 5-8%
- Example: At 87% vs 70%, you're saving approximately $1,200 per 40ft container

**Related Workflow:** Step 1.6.1 - Shipment Creation (Grouping Logic)

### 4. **System Alerts**
**Current Value:** 6 active alerts  
**Trend:** Critical

**What it means:**
- Number of anomalies, exceptions, or critical issues detected by the ORION system
- Alerts are categorized by severity:
  - **Danger (Red):** Requires immediate action (e.g., ETD < 48hrs, over-production)
  - **Warning (Amber):** Needs attention but not urgent (e.g., inventory low, EDI pending)

**Business Impact:**
- Proactive issue detection prevents downstream problems
- Alerts trigger automated notifications to relevant stakeholders
- Unresolved alerts can cascade into delays, stockouts, or excess inventory

**Related Workflow:** All steps - System-wide monitoring

## Summary

The ORION Dashboard transforms raw supply chain data into actionable intelligence, enabling:
- **Proactive Issue Resolution:** Detect anomalies before they become crises
- **Data-Driven Decisions:** KPIs guide resource allocation and prioritization
- **Workflow Transparency:** Activity feed provides full audit trail
- **Cross-Functional Collaboration:** Shared visibility across Ops, Suppliers, and Executives

All demo data is realistic and reflects actual supply chain scenarios, making it ideal for training, demonstrations, and system validation.
